Academy structure

Built around your Discord workflow.

Affiliates apply, join Discord, and the owner manually handles GoAffPro setup. The site explains the process and stores the playbook.

01

Apply

New affiliates submit interest and are directed into your Discord-based onboarding.

02

Owner Setup

The owner creates the affiliate’s GoAffPro account and provides their referral link.

03

Train

Affiliates review expectations, product messaging, content rules, and posting basics.

04

Create

You manually add hooks, video ideas, scripts, b-roll ideas, and captions to the vault.

05

Review

Affiliates can route content through Discord before posting when approval is needed.

06

Scale

Winning ideas can be moved into approved templates once you know they perform.
