Academy structure

Built around your actual affiliate workflow.

Affiliates apply, join Discord, and the owner manually handles GoAffPro setup. The site explains the process, stores the playbook, and gives affiliates one place to check updates.

01

Apply

New affiliates submit interest and are routed into Discord-based onboarding.

02

Owner Setup

The owner creates the affiliate’s GoAffPro account and provides their referral link.

03

Train

Affiliates review expectations, messaging, content rules, and posting basics.

04

Use a Skool-like feed for announcements, reminders, wins, updates, and prompts.

05

Create

You manually add hooks, video ideas, scripts, b-roll, captions, and CTAs.

06

Scale

Winning ideas can be moved into approved templates once you know they perform.
