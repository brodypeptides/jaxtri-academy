How setup works

Manual approval first. GoAffPro second.

The owner reviews the affiliate, creates their GoAffPro account, and sends their referral details manually.

  1. Step 1Affiliate joins Discord or submits the form you choose.
  2. Step 2Owner reviews the affiliate and confirms they are accepted.
  3. Step 3Owner creates the GoAffPro affiliate account.
  4. Step 4Affiliate receives their login/link and starts training.
